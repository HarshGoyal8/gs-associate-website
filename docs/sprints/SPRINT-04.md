# GS Associate Website — Sprint 04

> **Sprint:** 04 — Company & Conversion Pages
> **Version:** 0.4.0
> **Status:** Planned
> **Estimated Duration:** 5–7 Days

---

# Sprint Goal

Complete the public-facing website by building the remaining pages that strengthen trust, provide business information, and convert visitors into enquiries.

---

# Sprint Objectives

* Develop catalogue experience
* Build About page
* Build Contact page
* Create Dealer page
* Improve lead generation
