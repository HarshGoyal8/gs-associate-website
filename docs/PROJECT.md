# Project Overview

GS Associate is a leading wholesale distributor of plywood, laminates, HDHMR boards, flush doors, Boilo, FSP boards, and interior materials based in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh.

The objective of this project is to build a premium digital platform that reflects the scale, professionalism, and trustworthiness of GS Associate while creating a strong foundation for future business operations.

This is **not** a brochure website.

It is the beginning of a scalable business platform that will evolve into a complete ecosystem for product management, dealer management, enquiries, catalogues, and business operations.

---

# Vision

Create the most premium digital experience in the plywood and interior materials industry in India.

The website should position GS Associate as:

* A trusted wholesale partner
* A premium interior materials supplier
* A technology-driven business
* A market leader across Central India

The design should communicate quality, trust, and scale before the visitor reads a single line of text.

---

# Mission

Build a modern, elegant, high-performance website that:

* Creates an exceptional first impression
* Generates high-quality dealer enquiries
* Showcases products beautifully
* Makes catalogues easily accessible

* Serves as the foundation for future business software

# Design Philosophy

The website should feel like a premium interior brand rather than a traditional hardware supplier.

Visitors should immediately associate GS Associate with quality, reliability, elegance, and scale.

---

## Brand Personality

* Premium
* Elegant
* Confident
* Modern
* Trustworthy
* Minimal
* Sophisticated

---

## Avoid

* Cluttered layouts
* Bright flashy colors
* Generic templates
* Stock hardware-store appearance
* Excessive animations
* Cheap visual effects

---

# Design Principles

* White space is a feature.
* Typography is more important than decoration.
* Photography is more important than icons.
* Quality over quantity.
* Every section must have a purpose.
* Every interaction should feel smooth.
* Mobile experience is equally important as desktop.
