# GS Associate Website — Product Roadmap

> **Version:** 1.0
> **Status:** Active Roadmap
> **Last Updated:** June 2026

---

# Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term vision and development plan for the GS Associate digital platform.

It provides a structured path from a premium marketing website to a complete business management platform.

The roadmap helps prioritize development, prevent feature creep, and ensure that every release moves the project closer to its long-term vision.

---

# Product Vision

The end goal is **not** simply to build a website.

The long-term objective is to create the central digital platform that powers GS Associate's business operations, including:

* Product Management
* Catalogue Management
* Dealer Management
* Lead Generation
* CRM
* Inventory
* Analytics
* Order Management

Every release should move toward this vision.

---

# Development Strategy

The project will be developed incrementally.

Each version must:

* Deliver business value
* Be production-ready
* Maintain high code quality
* Improve user experience
* Remain scalable

No feature should be added unless it aligns with the long-term vision.

# Prioritisation Framework

Every new feature should be evaluated using the following order:

1. Business Impact
2. User Value
3. Long-Term Scalability
4. Development Complexity
5. Maintenance Cost

Features that provide high business impact with low maintenance should always be prioritised.

---

# Guiding Principle

The roadmap is a living document.

Features may evolve, priorities may change, and new opportunities may emerge.

However, one principle remains constant:

Every release should move GS Associate closer to becoming the leading digital platform for the plywood and interior materials industry rather than simply adding more pages to a website.
